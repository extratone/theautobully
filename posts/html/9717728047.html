<p>The Swedes were great at building cars<br/> for years.<br/> Designs were quirky, fun, and edgy. But<br/> Then all our Swedish car love turned to tears<br/> When SAAB&rsquo;s finances got stabbed in the gut.</p> <p>Where now are our 9-3&rsquo;s, 9-5&rsquo;s, and all<br/> Those other wond'rous cars they have brought to us?<br/> Where will we find such driving joy this fall,<br/> When autumn leaves excuse our driving lust?</p> <p>If winter&rsquo;s snows blanket our roads in white,<br/> And beg our hooning urge to dance and slide,<br/> What Swedish carriage will reflect the light<br/> That shines on snow thrown by throttle applied?</p> <p>Into that long good night our SAAB must go.<br/> And now the Swedes hang all hope on Volvo.</p>
— Poetry mourning the current Swedish automaker tragedy.  (Source below.)
